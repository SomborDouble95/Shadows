When Shadows Fall

by Arjun

You turn away
As the daylight fades,
And the long dark hours,
Call out your name.

But I'm close beside you,
A shelter from fears,
A hand to catch,
Each falling tear.

I'll bare my own heart,
No mask, no guise,
Stay with me now,
Stay through the night.

When morning breaks,
With silver skies,
We'll find our hope,
In the newborn light.

Red and Gold,
the morning sighs,
As the sun breaks free,
beneath the sky.

Put your hand in mine,
a trembling thread,
I'll anchor you,
through the winds ahead.

Stay with me now,
don't drift away,
The frost will lift,
the cold will fade.

In it's place,
a warmth will grow,
A fire within,
a love to show.
